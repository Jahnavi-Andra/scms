Avant Garde Sports Club was formed in 1998 in a small scale with 10 boys and one coach. The number and participants rose to 25 and two coaches respectively. In the subsequent years, many participants were enrolled and additional coaches were engaged. Today the coaching is being conducted at Race Course and Chekpauk Ground, Chennai. The strength of the Academy has gone up and coaching being given by 15 coaches who have played sports in all level. Scientific coaching methods are being given for physical exercises. More than 600 players have been trained since its inception. Many players have represented higher level of game Discipline is the sole motto of the Academy. The players are being taught to the art of living and helping the needy. Many state level players have come to the academy and appreciated the activities which is an unique feature. Many plans are ahead to produce laurels. In addition to the coaching activities, the boys are playing in the various league matches of Tamilnadu Sports Association and National Sports Association. Steps are being taken to inculcate good knowledge of the game.
